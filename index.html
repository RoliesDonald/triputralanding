About Us

    Our Humble Beginnings

    PT. Triputra Jaya Internasional was established in 2016, in Jakarta, Indonesia with the main purpose to support local commodities.

  • 2019

    We Are Born

    After few years as PT. Triputra Jaya Internasional grew in size and businesses developed until abroad, We made PT. Triputra Jaya Internasional officially established.

    Today
