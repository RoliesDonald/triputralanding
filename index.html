About Us

We will be next Super Company

  • 2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    PT. Triputra Jaya Internasional was established in 2016, in Jakarta, Indonesia with the main purpose to support local commodities. After few years, at 2019, PT. Triputra Jaya Internasional grew in size and business developed until Hongkong and Germany, they made PT. Triputra Jaya Internasional officially established. Today, PT. Triputra Jaya Internasional interest in local and general trading, export – import, procurement also retail distribution. Our focus mainly in Agro commodities, Coffee, Timber, Fishery, Chemical, Garment, Food Beverage, Cosmetics, also Consumer goods.

  • 2019

    We Are Born

    With our expertise in both local and international trading, we assure best quality service and products, we possess variety knowledge to provide availability from the best sources. Our practical paradigms refer to global B2B Trade, highly specialized in some goods with strong logistic. We guarantee faster distribution, computing, and excellent marketing.

  • 2020

    Transition to Full Service

    PT. Triputra Jaya Internasional head quarter is located in Jakarta, Indonesia, and have several Representative Offices in domestic; Surabaya, Bali, Medan, Makasar. However, its other affiliate companies scattered in several countries as well; such as Hongkong and Germany.

Vision

To be the one of local and international general trading company which have the strengths in providing comprehensive products and services in the most reliable and cost-effective manners. We want to continue our legacy as the most ethical trade partner of choice and known as the premier trade partner that fosters new ideas, innovation, and growth but also customers best choice. We will earn our clients trust and loyalty by continuous improvement driven by our core values.

Mission

To operate the best general trading business that create the values for our employees, investors and communities. We established long lasting relationship with our clients by gaining their trust through excellent performance by every member of our team.

Core Value

Integrity

We generate honesty, transparency and consistency in word and deed in every business lines, with highest ethical standards.

Engagement

Full engagement of all team members is our greatest values.

Passion

We show pride, enthusiasm and dedication in everything we do. We are committed to deliver values to our clients.
